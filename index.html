Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 2023

Water Sort Deluxe (the "App") is a mobile game developed by Amos Banda ("we", "us", or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

No Personal Information: We do not collect, store, or share any personal information that can identify you (such as your name, email address, phone number, or location).

Local Data Storage: The App stores your game progress, coin balance, stars earned, and levels completed locally on your device using your browser's local storage. This data never leaves your device.

2. Permissions Used

The App requires the following permissions to function correctly:

  • Vibration: To provide tactile feedback when an incorrect move is made.
  • Local Storage: To save your game records and settings.

3. Third-Party Services

Currently, Water Sort Deluxe does not use any third-party analytics, advertising networks, or external services that collect data from your device.

4. Children's Privacy

Our game is designed to be family-friendly and safe for all ages. Since we do not collect any personal information, we do not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
