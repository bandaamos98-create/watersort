Welcome to Water Sort Deluxe. Your privacy is not just a policy for us; it's a priority. This document outlines how we handle information in our mobile game developed by Amos Banda.

Information Collection

We do not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII).

Device Local Storage

Game progress, coins, and settings are saved only on your device. This data never leaves your phone.

  • Local Storage Access: Necessary to save your progress and high scores locally.

Third-Party Services

We value a clean experience. Water Sort Deluxe does not use third-party analytics, advertising trackers, or external APIs that collect data from your device.

Family Safety

Our game is designed to be safe for all ages. Because we do not collect personal data, we are fully compliant with privacy standards for children under 13.
